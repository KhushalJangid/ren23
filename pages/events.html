*6 players per team.
*Game will be of 5 overs a side.
*Registration fees paid is non refundable.
*A player or a team can be disqualified under indisciplinary acts on the ground.
BATTING RULES

*HIT OVER THE BOUNDARY - Its not six:If the batsman hits direct over the boundary,he is out, though if the ball crosses the boundary line grounded, its a four.

*TRY BALL - A player cannot be given out on the first ball of the innings i.e. the very first ball of innings played by the batsman is a free hit.
